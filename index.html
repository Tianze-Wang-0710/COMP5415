Introduction to Chinese Tea Culture

Tea culture is an important part of Chinese traditional culture, and its history dates back thousands of years. From ancient times to the present, tea culture has always played an important role in people's daily lives, forming a rich historical heritage and cultural connotation. In China, tea is not only a daily drink for people, but also a way of socializing and a manifestation of spiritual cultivation.
